1. What does fr stand for?
fr stands for fractional unit and it represents a portion of the available space.

2. How many columns are created?
Three columns are created.

3. What happens when screen size is reduced?
The columns shrink proportionally to fit the screen.

4. Do items overflow or adjust?
The items adjust automatically within the available space.

5. Why is fr better than px for layouts?
fr is responsive and adapts to screen size, while px is fixed.
