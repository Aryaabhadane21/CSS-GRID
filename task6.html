1. What are grid lines?
Grid lines are the lines that divide the grid into columns and rows.

2. How many columns does the item span?
The item spans two columns.

3. What happens to other items?
Other items move to the next available grid cells.

4. Can two items overlap?
Yes, if placed on the same grid lines.

5. Where is column spanning used in websites?
Column spanning is used for headers, banners, and featured sections.
